About this guide¶

This is the technical reference guide for GOV.UK Verify.

This guide contains information to help teams who are building secure digital services to integrate with GOV.UK Verify, and should be used together with the GOV.UK Verify Onboarding Guide. This guide will be continually improved. Check the version history for the latest changes to the guide.

Who this guide is for¶

This guide is for technical delivery teams who are building secure digital services that integrate with GOV.UK Verify.
